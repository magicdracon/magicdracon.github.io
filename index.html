H.A.C.K.S

As the President of H.A.C.K.S (Hurricane Association for Computer Koding and Science) for two years, I've led a club dedicated to exploring various facets of computer technology. From game development to programming and IT, H.A.C.K.S provides a collaborative space for students to grow their skills and engage in meaningful projects.


Our club has achieved notable successes, including:

  1. Securing first place in speed at the State Ken Garff eSport coding tournament.
  2. Winning the Ken Garff eSport Southern Utah Tournament for coding, earning a $500 scholarship from Utah Tech.
  3. Developing websites for local businesses and providing IT services to the elderly, making a positive impact in the community.
  4. Organizing field trips to tech companies like Pluralsight and college visits to Neumont, giving members valuable industry insights.

    5. Leading H.A.C.K.S has been a rewarding experience that’s reinforced my passion for technology and leadership. By supporting members' interests and providing resources, I've helped create a thriving community of future tech leaders. I am incredibly grateful to have the privilege of being the president of H.A.C.K.S
